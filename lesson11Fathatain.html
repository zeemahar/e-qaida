Lesson No. 23

Fathatain (Double Fatha)

(Page 23)
  1. Before we did all the movements in its single form now, we will have all the mentioned symbols in its double form.
  2. The symbols Two Fatha (Fathatain), two Kasra (Kasratain) and two Dhamma (Double Dhammatain) are called Tanween, because the Noon sound is being produced when we pronounce it i.e ( ـًـ ) ( ـٍـ ) ( ـٌـ )
  3. While pronouncing Tanween, the sound of the letter should not be prolonged such as بٌ should be read بن not بون
  4. The rules of Tanween will be mentioned gradually in upcoming topics.
  1. Fathatain
    1. The first type of Tanween is Double Fatha/Fathatain.
    2. If there is Double Fatha/ on the last letter, it will be pronounced with Alif instead of Tanween while, stopping on the last letter.
