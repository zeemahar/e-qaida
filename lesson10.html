Lesson No. 10

Leen Letters

(Page 21)

There are two Leen Letters.

  1. If there is Fatha (ـَـ) before و sakin or ى sakin, it is called soft letter or Leen Letters. For Example: يَوْ، تَوْ and تي، ثي etc.
  2. Leen Letters should be pronounced softly without stretching.
  3. The difference between Maddah Letters and Leen Letters, must be identified carefully ,considering in sound.
  4. If the Leen letter appear in the end of word, it could be prolonged up to 3 /4 Alif, while, stopping on the last letter.
