Compound Letters

(Page 04)
  1. A letter could be changed when it comes together with another letter.
  2. Read all the letters separately and try to know the way that how different letters are being formed and joined together, keeping in view the previous lessons, considering in pronunciation and shapes.
  3. ء ا د ذ ر ز و ء These letters will never be connected in the beginning, even though Hamza cannot be enjoined in the ending.
