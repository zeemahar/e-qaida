Lesson No. 11

Dhammatain (Double Dhamma)

(Page 27)
  1. Dhammatain
    1. The double Dhamma always appeared on the letter.
    2. If there is Double Dhamma on the last letter, it will be pronounced with Sakoon instead of Tanween while, stopping on the last letter.
    3. If you still face problems in recognizing the movement then revise all the lessons mentioned before.
