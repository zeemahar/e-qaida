Lesson No. 18

Muqat,aat Letters (Disjoined or Disconnected Letters)

(Page 45)
    1. These letters usually come in the beginning of some Quranic surah and will have to read separately and has to be recited as they are written.
    2. f Madda Letter followed by sakoon Letter in Muqattiat Letters,then it will be stretched up to 3 Alif and it is called Madd Lazim Harfi Mukhaffaf,as we have Laam Meem where Meem sakin comes after. Example: المّ in ميم
    3. If Madda Letter followed by shadda Letter while pronouncing Muqattiat Letters,then it will be prolonged up to 3 Alif and it is called Madd Lazim Harfi Muthaqqal,as we have Meem sound in Alif aam Meem.Example: المّ in لامميم
    4. The Madd in the letter عينof كهيعصis called Madd Lazim Leen.
