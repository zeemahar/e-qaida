Lesson No. 16

Idghaam-e-Shafawi, Ikhfaa-e-Shafawi & Izhaar-e-Shafawi

(Page 42)
    1. There are three rules of Meem Sakin.
    2. If Meem Sakin followed by another letter Meem then there will be merging sound and it is called Idgham Shafawi.
    3. If Meem Sakin followed by letter Baa then there will be Ikhfa (Hiding) and it is called Ikhfa Shafawi.
    4. If Meem Sakin followed by any letter other than Meem and Baa then there will be Izhar and it is called Izhar Shafawi.
