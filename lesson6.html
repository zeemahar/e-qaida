Jazm

  1. Jazm is placed on the letter.
  2. The letters having sign of Jazm ـْـ is called Sakin/Majzum
  3. Jazm does not have its own sound.
  4. The letter, having sign of Jazm, is always being recited with first letter.
  5. In stopping condition we do not need to change the Jazm, when it appears on the last letter.
